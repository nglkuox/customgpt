## What's the actual idea here?

The problem with generic AI tools like ChatGPT is that they don't know your business. They'll give confidently wrong answers because they're drawing from everything and nothing at the same time. CustomGPT.ai flips that: you feed it your content, it builds a retrieval layer on top, and every response gets traced back to a real source in your knowledge base. It's called RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), which sounds technical but basically means "it actually checks before it answers."

Connect your website, upload your PDFs, link Google Drive, Notion, Zendesk, HubSpot — whatever lives in your world — and the platform builds an AI that speaks in your voice, about your stuff, accurately.

The result? An agent that handles customer queries 24/7, helps new employees find policies instantly, resolves support tickets before a human has to touch them, and never goes on vacation.

## The honest downsides

A few things worth knowing before you commit:

- **Price.** Multiple users on Capterra and Software Advice noted that the monthly plans run higher than competing tools, especially for smaller teams or early-stage startups.
- **Query limits are account-wide.** The 1,000 or 5,000 monthly queries are shared across all your agents, not per bot. Run a busy site and you'll hit this faster than expected.
- **Premium features are locked to higher tiers.** White-labeling, auto-sync, and SharePoint integration all require Premium ($449/month billed annually) or above.
